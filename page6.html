LABORATORY HIGH SCHOOL

The building of the high school students, also know as "kuta ng mga labhigh", was named after GAVINO M. CARPIO, a native of Hagonoy, Bulacan. He is the first president then Bulacan College of Arts and Trades (BCAT). Carpio Hall was constructed during his term of office (July 1, 1968 - December 31, 1973)

The building was renovated and repaired throught the benevolence of Bulacan Governor Josefina M. Dela Cruz.
